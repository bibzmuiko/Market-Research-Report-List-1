<p><p>The additives for printing inks market is categorized into organic and inorganic types. Organic additives are derived from carbon-containing compounds and are often used for improving ink properties such as flow, adhesion, and drying time. They enhance color vibrancy and stability in various environments. In contrast, inorganic additives are mineral-based and contribute to durability, opacity, and UV resistance. Both types are essential for formulating high-performance inks suitable for different printing techniques and substrates.</p></p>
