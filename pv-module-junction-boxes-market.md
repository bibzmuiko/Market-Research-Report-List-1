<p><p>The PV module junction box market is primarily segmented into crystalline silicon and amorphous silicon junction boxes. Crystalline silicon junction boxes are used in traditional solar panels, offering high efficiency and durability, making them popular in various applications. In contrast, amorphous silicon junction boxes are associated with thin-film solar technologies, known for their lightweight and flexible properties. While crystalline silicon dominates the market due to its established performance, amorphous silicon is gaining traction for niche applications requiring adaptability and lower production costs.</p></p>
