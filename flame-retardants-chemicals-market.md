<p><p>The flame retardants chemicals market is categorized mainly into non-halogenated and halogenated flame retardants. Non-halogenated flame retardants, made from phosphorus, nitrogen, or other elements, are known for their environmentally friendly attributes and reduced toxic emissions during combustion. In contrast, halogenated flame retardants contain chlorine or bromine and are effective at lower concentrations but raise concerns regarding toxicity and environmental impact. Both types are widely used in textiles, electronics, and construction to enhance fire safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
