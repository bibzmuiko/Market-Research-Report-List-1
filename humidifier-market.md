<p><p>The humidifier market comprises various types, including ultrasonic and evaporative humidifiers, along with other variants. Ultrasonic humidifiers use high-frequency vibrations to create a fine mist, providing quiet and energy-efficient operation. Evaporative humidifiers utilize a fan to draw air through a wet wick or filter, promoting natural evaporation and humidity control. Other types may include steam vaporizers and impeller humidifiers, each with distinct mechanisms and features, catering to different consumer needs and preferences in indoor air quality management.</p></p>
