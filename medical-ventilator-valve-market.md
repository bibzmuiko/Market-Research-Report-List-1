<p><p>The medical ventilator valve market includes several types, each serving specific functions. Solenoid valves are electrically operated devices that control airflow by opening and closing rapidly, essential for precise ventilation control. Proportional valves offer variable airflow regulation, allowing for smoother, more adaptable patient support during mechanical ventilation. Additionally, other valves might include pressure relief or check valves, which ensure safety and efficiency in the ventilation process. Together, these components play a crucial role in enhancing patient care in respiratory therapies.</p></p>
