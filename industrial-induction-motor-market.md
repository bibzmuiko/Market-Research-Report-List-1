<p><strong>The Industrial Induction Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Squirrel-Cage</li><li>Slip-Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial induction motor market is primarily categorized into two types: squirrel-cage and slip-ring motors. Squirrel-cage motors are commonly used for their robustness, efficiency, and low maintenance, making them ideal for constant speed applications. In contrast, slip-ring motors are designed to handle variable load conditions, offering better torque control at startup and during operation, which makes them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types serve diverse industrial needs, influencing their market demand and growth prospects.</p></p>
