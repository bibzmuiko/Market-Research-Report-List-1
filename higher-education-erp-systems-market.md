<p><p>Higher Education ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) systems are categorized into two main market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises ERP systems require institutions to host and maintain software on their own servers, offering greater control and customization but necessitating significant IT resources. Conversely, cloud-based ERP systems are hosted by third-party providers, allowing institutions to access services via the internet. This model offers scalability, lower initial costs, and reduced maintenance responsibilities, making it increasingly popular among educational institutions seeking flexibility and efficiency.</p></p>
