<p><strong>The Exogenous Creatinine Measurement Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jaffe's Kinetic Method</li><li>Enzymatic Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Exogenous Creatinine Measurement Kits market primarily includes two types: Jaffe's Kinetic Method and the Enzymatic Method. The Jaffe's Kinetic Method utilizes a colorimetric reaction between creatinine and alkaline picrate, providing a fast and cost-effective measurement, but can be affected by interfering substances. In contrast, the Enzymatic Method employs enzymatic reactions for higher specificity and accuracy, minimizing interference from other compounds. Both methods serve essential roles in clinical diagnostics and monitoring renal function, catering to different laboratory needs and preferences.</p></p>
