<p><ul><li>Limit Fermentation</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low alcohol beer market primarily encompasses two types: limit fermentation and dealcoholization methods. Limit fermentation involves controlling the fermentation process to produce beer with lower alcohol content directly, often by using specific yeast strains or temperature control. In contrast, dealcoholization involves brewing regular beer and subsequently removing alcohol through various techniques such as evaporation or membrane filtration. Both methods cater to the growing consumer demand for healthier beverage options while maintaining the taste and experience of traditional beer.</p></p>
