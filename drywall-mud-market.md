<p><strong>The Drywall Mud Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quick-Setting or "Hot" Mud</li><li>Pre-Mixed Drywall Mud</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drywall mud market features two primary types: Quick-Setting or "Hot" Mud and Pre-Mixed Drywall Mud. Quick-Setting Mud is ideal for projects requiring fast drying times, allowing for quicker application and finishing, typically used in repair or patchwork tasks. Conversely, Pre-Mixed Mud is ready to use right out of the bucket, offering ease for larger projects and less experienced users. While it takes longer to dry, it provides a smoother finish, making it preferred for extensive drywall installations.</p></p>
