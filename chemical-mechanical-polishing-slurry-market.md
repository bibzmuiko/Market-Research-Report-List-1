<p><p>The Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) slurry market comprises three primary types: alumina slurry, colloidal silica slurry, and ceria slurries. Alumina slurry is known for its efficiency in polishing harder materials, making it suitable for various applications in semiconductor and optical industries. Colloidal silica slurry offers a finer finish and is often used for CMP in semiconductor manufacturing. Ceria slurries excel in polishing glass substrates and are popular in the optical sector due to their superior planarization capabilities.</p></p>
