<p><p>High Purity Isobutylene (HPIB) is produced through various methods, each catering to specific market needs. The MTBE Decomposition Method involves breaking down methyl tert-butyl ether to extract isobutylene. The C4 Fraction Separation Method focuses on separating isobutylene from a stream of C4 hydrocarbons. The Isobutane Dehydrogenation Method converts isobutane directly into isobutylene using high temperatures and catalysts. Other methods may include thermal cracking or oxidative dehydrogenation, providing additional alternatives for HPIB production in diverse applications.</p></p>
