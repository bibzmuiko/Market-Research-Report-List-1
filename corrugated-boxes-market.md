<p><strong>The Corrugated Boxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Corrugated</li><li>Double Corrugated</li><li>Triple Corrugated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrugated boxes market consists of three main types: single, double, and triple corrugated boxes. Single corrugated boxes, made from one layer of fluted paper sandwiched between two linerboards, are lightweight and cost-effective, making them ideal for lighter products. Double corrugated boxes feature two layers of fluted paper, offering enhanced strength and protection for moderate-weight items. Triple corrugated boxes, comprising three layers of fluting, provide maximum durability and are suited for heavy-duty applications, such as shipping fragile goods.</p></p>
