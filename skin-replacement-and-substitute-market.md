<p><p>The skin replacement and substitute market encompasses various products for wound healing and skin restoration. Allografts are human tissue transplants, while xenografts are derived from animal sources. Cultured epithelial autografts involve lab-grown skin using the patient's cells. Collagen scaffolds provide structural support for regeneration, and acellular dermal matrices offer a biological framework to facilitate healing. Amniotic membranes are sourced from placenta, providing a rich healing environment. Synthetic skin consists of engineered materials designed to mimic natural skin properties.</p></p>
