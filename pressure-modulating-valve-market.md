<p><p>The pressure modulating valve market includes types such as butterfly valves and ball valves, each serving unique applications in fluid control systems. Butterfly valves, characterized by a disc that rotates to regulate flow, are ideal for quick shut-off and space efficiency. Ball valves feature a spherical disc that allows for minimal flow resistance and precise control, making them suitable for on/off operations. Both types are essential in industries like water treatment, oil and gas, and HVAC, facilitating effective pressure modulation.</p></p>
