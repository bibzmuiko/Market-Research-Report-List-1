<p><p>The refractory magnesia market is categorized into three main types: Fused Magnesia, Dead Burned Magnesia, and Others. Fused Magnesia is produced by melting high-purity magnesite in electric furnaces, resulting in a dense, high-performance material used in steelmaking and foundries. Dead Burned Magnesia is obtained by calcining magnesite at high temperatures, forming a stable product ideal for refractory applications. The "Others" category includes various processed magnesia products tailored for specific industrial uses, expanding the market’s versatility.</p></p>
