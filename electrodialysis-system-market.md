<p><strong>The Electrodialysis System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Electrodialysis</li><li>Batch Electrodialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrodialysis System Market is primarily categorized into two types: Continuous Electrodialysis and Batch Electrodialysis. Continuous Electrodialysis operates in a steady-state mode, allowing for the constant processing of feed solutions, making it suitable for large-scale applications with high efficiency and productivity. In contrast, Batch Electrodialysis processes solutions in discrete batches, providing flexibility and control, ideal for smaller volumes or specific applications. Both systems utilize electric fields to drive ion-selective separation through membranes, enhancing water treatment and desalination processes.</p></p>
