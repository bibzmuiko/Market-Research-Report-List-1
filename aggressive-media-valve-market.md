<p><p>The Aggressive Media Valve Market is categorized into two main types: 2 Way Valves and 3 Way Valves. 2 Way Valves function by controlling the flow of aggressive media in a single direction, essential for on-off operations. Conversely, 3 Way Valves provide more versatility by allowing the inlet and outlet to connect in multiple configurations, enabling flow direction control and mixing of different fluids. Both types are crucial in industries managing corrosive substances, ensuring safe and efficient fluid handling.</p></p>
