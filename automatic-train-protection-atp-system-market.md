<p><strong>The Automatic Train Protection (ATP) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional ATP</li><li>Digital ATP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Train Protection (ATP) system market is divided into two main types: Conventional ATP and Digital ATP. Conventional ATP systems primarily rely on trackside equipment and analog signals to monitor train speeds and prevent collisions or derailments. In contrast, Digital ATP systems utilize advanced digital technologies, including onboard computers and communication networks, to enhance safety, enable real-time data exchange, and facilitate more sophisticated control mechanisms. Both types aim to improve railway safety and operational efficiency, but through different technological approaches.</p></p>
