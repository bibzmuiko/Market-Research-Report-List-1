<p><p>Thermal circuit breakers are essential safety devices designed to automatically interrupt electrical circuits in response to overcurrent conditions, preventing damage to equipment and reducing fire hazards. <p><p>Thermal circuit breakers are categorized into several types based on their mounting and integration features. Integral type breakers are integrated directly into devices for compactness. PCB mounting breakers are designed for direct installation on printed circuit boards, ensuring efficient use of space. Snap-in mounting breakers allow easy installation and replacement in panels. Panel mounting types are attached externally to panels, providing accessible reset options. Plug-in type breakers offer flexibility for easy replacement and maintenance, adapting to various applications.</p></p>
