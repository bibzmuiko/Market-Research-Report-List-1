<p><p>Glycol dehydration units (GDU) are categorized based on their processing capacity indicated in thousand standard cubic feet per day (MSCFD). Units under 60 MSCFD are typically used for smaller applications, focusing on moisture removal in low gas flow scenarios. Units between 60-100 MSCFD are suitable for medium-sized operations, offering increased efficiency. The 100-450 MSCFD range caters to larger installations, addressing higher gas volumes. Above 450 MSCFD units are designed for massive industrial applications, ensuring effective dehydration at considerable flow rates.</p></p>
