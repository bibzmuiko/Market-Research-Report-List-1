<p><ul><li>Crawler Drive</li><li>Wheel Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robotic pool cleaner market is primarily segmented into crawler drive and wheel drive types. Crawler drive models use tracks for enhanced traction on various surfaces, ensuring thorough cleaning in pools with irregular shapes or inclines. In contrast, wheel drive cleaners utilize wheels for movement, offering faster navigation and efficiency on flat surfaces. Both types have unique advantages, promoting effective debris removal and user convenience, which contribute to the growing demand for automated pool maintenance solutions.</p></p>
