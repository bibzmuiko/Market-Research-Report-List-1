<p><p>The industrial calcium sulfate market primarily consists of two types: powder and lump. Calcium sulfate powder is finely ground, offering high solubility and application versatility in industries such as construction, food, and agriculture. It is often used as a desiccant or in drywall production. Conversely, lump calcium sulfate is coarser and typically used in applications requiring bulk material, such as soil conditioning and cement formulation. Each form serves distinct industrial needs, influencing demand patterns across varying sectors.</p></p>
