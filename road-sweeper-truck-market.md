<p><p>The road sweeper truck market comprises several types, including dry road sweeper trucks, wet road sweeper trucks, and other specialized units. Dry road sweepers utilize mechanical brushes and suction to collect dust and debris without the use of water, making them ideal for arid regions. Wet road sweepers, on the other hand, spray water to control dust while sweeping, suitable for urban environments with heavy foot and vehicle traffic. Other types may include vacuum or regenerative air sweepers, catering to unique cleaning needs.</p></p>
