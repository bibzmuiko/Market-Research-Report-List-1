Key market types include Rod Lift, which uses steel rods to bring oil to the surface; Electric Submersible Pumps (ESP), which utilize electrical energy for efficient fluid movement; Progressive Cavity Pumps (PCP), designed for high-viscosity fluids; Hydraulic Pumps that leverage hydraulic pressure; Gas Lift, which injects gas to reduce fluid density; and Plunger Lift, which uses a plunger for intermittent lifting. Each system is tailored for specific well conditions and production requirements.</p></p>
