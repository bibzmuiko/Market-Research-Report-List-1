<p><p>The flexible food packaging market comprises various materials, including PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), PE (Polyethylene), PP (Polypropylene), and other specialized materials. PET is widely used for its clarity and barrier properties, making it ideal for beverages and ready-to-eat meals. PE offers excellent moisture resistance and is often used in bags and wraps. PP is valued for its strength and heat resistance, favored in microwaveable containers. Other materials include biodegradable and compostable options, catering to sustainability trends in the food industry.</p></p>
