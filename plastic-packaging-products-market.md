<p><p>The plastic packaging products market comprises two main types: rigid and flexible plastic packaging. Rigid plastic packaging includes containers, bottles, and trays that maintain their shape and provide sturdy protection for products. It's commonly used for items like beverages and food. Flexible plastic packaging, on the other hand, consists of materials that can be easily bent or warped, such as pouches and films, offering versatility and lightweight solutions for packaging snacks, personal care items, and more. Together, they address diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
