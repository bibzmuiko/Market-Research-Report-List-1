<p><p>The bonding neodymium magnet market is primarily categorized into two types: injection magnets and pressed magnets. Injection magnets involve mixing neodymium powder with a polymer and injecting the mixture into molds to create complex shapes, resulting in high precision and versatility. Pressed magnets, on the other hand, involve compacting the powder into a desired shape under pressure, followed by sintering for enhanced magnetic properties. Both methods cater to diverse applications, balancing performance and manufacturing efficiency in the magnet industry.</p></p>
