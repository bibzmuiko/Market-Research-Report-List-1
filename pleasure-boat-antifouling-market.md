<p><p>The pleasure boat antifouling market is categorized primarily into two types: copper-based and copper-free antifouling paints. Conversely, copper-free types employ alternative biocides or non-toxic ingredients, catering to eco-conscious consumers while offering varying degrees of effectiveness against fouling. Both market segments address the need for maintaining hull performance, prolonging vessel lifespan, and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
