<p><ul><li>Emulsion</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorocarbon-based vacuum grease is categorized into two main types: emulsion and powder. Emulsion greases are typically stable mixtures of fluorocarbon compounds with water or other solvents, providing excellent lubricating properties while offering ease of application. Powdered fluorocarbon greases, on the other hand, consist of solid particles, delivering high thermal stability and resistance to chemical reactions. Both types serve critical roles in vacuum applications, ensuring optimal performance and longevity of equipment by minimizing friction and wear under extreme conditions.</p></p>
