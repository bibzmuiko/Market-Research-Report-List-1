Pure coatings consist of single-component systems that offer rapid curing and high resistance to chemicals and abrasion. In contrast, hybrid coatings combine polyurea or polyaspartic with epoxy elements, providing added flexibility and enhanced performance. Both types cater to diverse needs, ensuring protection and longevity for garage floors in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
