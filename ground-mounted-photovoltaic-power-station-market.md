<p><ul><li>Crystalline Silicon Photovoltaic Power Station</li><li>Thin Film Photovoltaic Power Station</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ground-mounted photovoltaic power stations can be categorized into two main types: crystalline silicon and thin film photovoltaic power stations. Crystalline silicon photovoltaic power stations utilize silicon-based solar cells known for their high efficiency and durability, making them the most common type in the market. Conversely, thin film photovoltaic power stations employ materials like cadmium telluride or amorphous silicon, offering flexibility and lower production costs, but typically with reduced efficiency. Both types play a significant role in renewable energy generation and sustainability efforts.</p></p>
