<p><ul><li>Emulsion PCR</li><li>Bridge Amplification</li><li>Single-molecule</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gene sequencing market types include Emulsion PCR, Bridge Amplification, and Single-molecule sequencing. Emulsion PCR involves creating microdroplets to amplify DNA, enhancing specificity and throughput. Bridge Amplification uses a solid surface and enables the simultaneous amplification of many DNA fragments, supporting high-density sequencing. Single-molecule sequencing allows for reading DNA strands without amplification, providing rapid results and reducing errors. Each method contributes to advancements in genomics, offering unique advantages for various applications in research and clinical diagnostics.</p></p>
